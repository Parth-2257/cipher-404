Cybersecurity Roadmap

1. Foundations

Essential knowledge and skills to build your cybersecurity foundation.

Networking Basics (IP, DNS, Ports, Protocols)
Operating Systems (Windows, Linux)
Programming (Python, C/C++, JavaScript)
Cybersecurity Basics (CIA Triad, Threats, Vulnerabilities)

2. Security Domains

Core areas of cybersecurity expertise and their key components.

Network Security: Firewalls, IDS/IPS, VPNs
Application Security: OWASP Top 10, Secure Coding
Cloud Security: AWS, Azure, IAM, Encryption
Endpoint Security: Antivirus, System Hardening
Web & API Security: Authentication, XSS, SQLi
Cryptography: Hashing, Encryption, SSL/TLS

3. Defensive Security (Blue Team)

Protecting and defending systems against cyber threats.

SOC Operations & SIEM (Splunk, ELK)
Incident Response & Digital Forensics
Threat Hunting & Intelligence (MITRE ATT&CK)
Governance, Risk & Compliance (NIST, ISO 27001)

4. Offensive Security (Red Team)

Ethical hacking and penetration testing methodologies.

Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing
Exploit Development & Privilege Escalation
Red Team Operations & Adversary Simulation
Reverse Engineering & Malware Analysis

5. Specialized Tracks

Focused career paths in cybersecurity.

Blue Team (Defense)
Red Team (Offense)
Cloud Security
GRC / Audit
Forensics & Malware Analysis

6. Certifications

Industry-recognized certifications to validate your skills.

Beginner: CompTIA Security+, CEH
Intermediate: eJPT, CySA+
Advanced: OSCP, CISSP, CISM

7. Projects & Practice

Hands-on experience to apply your knowledge.

Home Lab (TryHackMe, HackTheBox)
CTF Challenges
Vulnerability Assessments
Security Reports & Blogs
